What to Expect

Historical Significance: The Khajuraho Group of Monuments consists of Hindu and Jain temples built between 950-1050 CE by the Chandela dynasty. These temples are famous worldwide for their nagara-style architectural symbolism and erotic sculptures.

Photography Tips: The morning light offers the best conditions for photographing the intricate stone carvings. Professional cameras are allowed with an additional fee.

What to Wear: Comfortable walking shoes and modest clothing are recommended as you'll be visiting religious sites.

Local Souvenirs: Don't miss the opportunity to purchase handcrafted stone replicas, bronze statues, and local handicrafts from the artisan markets near the temple complex.
